ABOUT

The fastest hands from India meet the smartest mouth from China when the legendary harebrained criminal Jeffrey Josh teams up with the nerded out joker Andrew Sun in Peak Pursuit. When Chicago rookie cop, Raj Patel (Josh) finds himself fighting the corrupt Chicago Police Department due to his girlfriend being murdered by the Chief, the broken cop traverses Chicago to find redemption. Unhappy to have just got sued by his client, the self-designated genius, dardevil lawyer Jimmy Wu (Sun) teams up with Patel in a criminal alliance to gain back his riches. These two very different guys will stop at nothing, not even robbing a bank, to get back at the system... .poster { }
